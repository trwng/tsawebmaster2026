Resources You Should Know About

Three organizations making a huge difference in Gwinnett County right now

Food Security

Gwinnett County Food Bank

Providing food assistance to over 700 families each week. They run sorting drives, mobile pantries, and holiday meal programs throughout the county.

📍 Lawrenceville, GA 📞 (770) 963-6986
Education

Gwinnett Public Library

Free tutoring, digital literacy classes, ESL programs, and after-school support for students K-12.

📍 Multiple locations 🌐 gwinnettpl.org
Environment

Keep Gwinnett Beautiful

Beautifying parks, waterways, and neighborhoods through volunteer cleanups, tree plantings, and environmental education. Zero cost to join.

📍 Gwinnett-wide 🌐 keepgwinnettbeautiful.org
